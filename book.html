The Book

Still Here! Still Queer! Now What?

A Guide to LGBTQ+ Wellness and Wholeness

"To all those who did not make it this far. Rest in power."

This book started with a question I couldn't stop asking: what does it mean to be queer and middle-aged in a world that didn't expect you to get this far?

I spoke to fifty-one people — queer men, women, and non-binary folk aged 40 to 60 — and asked them everything. About identity, intimacy, shame, desire, ageing, belonging, loss, legacy, and what they want from the rest of their lives. Their answers were funny, heartbreaking, filthy, and hopeful, often in the same sentence.

This book weaves their voices together with psychology, philosophy, queer cultural history, and three original coaching frameworks designed specifically for us — because every self-help tool I'd ever seen was built for straight people and it showed.

Part I: Still Here!

Helps you look back without flinching. Who have you been? What did it cost? And what did you gain that you haven't given yourself credit for yet?

Part II: Still Queer!

Helps you feel what you've been avoiding. A guided return to the wholeness that was always there — underneath the masks, the armour, and the stories other people wrote about you.

Part III: Now What?

Helps you decide what happens next. A personal manifesto — your promise to yourself about how to live from here.
