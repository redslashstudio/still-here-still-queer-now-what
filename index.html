Come home to your whole self

To the person you were before they told you to be someone else

Still Here! Still Queer! Now What? draws on psychology, philosophy, and fifty-one conversations with queer people aged forty to sixty — not to tell you what’s wrong, but to help you remember what was always right.

Understanding where you are

Looking back without flinching. Who have you been? What did it cost? And what did you gain that you haven’t given yourself credit for yet?

Reconnecting with who you really are

A guided return to the wholeness that was always there — underneath the masks, the armour, and the stories other people wrote about you.

Deciding what happens next

On your own terms. A personal manifesto — your promise to yourself about how to live from here.

The Book

Still Here! Still Queer! Now What?

A Guide to LGBTQ+ Wellness and Wholeness

This book started with a question Paul couldn’t stop asking: what does it mean to be queer and middle-aged in a world that didn’t expect you to get this far?

Drawing on fifty-one conversations, psychology, philosophy, and queer cultural history, it’s part love letter, part rallying cry — and the tools to reconnect with a wholeness that was always yours.
